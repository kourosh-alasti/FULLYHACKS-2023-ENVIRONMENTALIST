Welcome to Project Green Tus

Why Project Green Tusk?

Project Green Tusk has two aspects in its meaning. The Tusk symbolizes power and strength, both of which we want to strive for in our project to educate the youth on our Environment. While Project Green refers to the term Green Thumb, the natural talent to grow plants.

Who are we?

We're a group of students who are passionate about the environment and want to make a difference.

About Environmental Quiz

Environmental Quiz is a website designed to help users learn about environmental issues through interactive quizzes. Our quizzes cover a range of topics such as pollution, conservation, and sustainability.
