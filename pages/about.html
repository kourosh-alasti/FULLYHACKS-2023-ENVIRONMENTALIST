About Us

Welcome

We're California State Fullerton Students!

Who are we?

We're a group of students who are passionate about the environment and want to make a difference.

About Environmental Quiz

Environmental Quiz is a website designed to help users learn about environmental issues through interactive quizzes. Our quizzes cover a range of topics such as pollution, conservation, and sustainability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate and raise awareness about environmental issues in a fun and engaging way. By taking our quizzes, users can test their knowledge and learn new facts about the environment. At the same time, we strive to make a difference by supporting environmental charities and promoting sustainability.

That's why a portion of our revenue is donated to environmental charities that are making a real difference, such as planting trees, cleaning up the ocean, and protecting endangered animals.

Why is this important?

We believe that knowledge is power, and that by educating ourselves and others about environmental issues, we can make a positive impact in our community and beyond. We want to inspire people to take action and make sustainable choices that can help protect our planet for generations to come.
